Informations :

This tracker is not fully done. It's still useable and should sync on realtime !
It has been done to follow a race of a streamer while the community enter informations for everyone who join the stream and wonder what have been checked and where was found any item !

Currently, it work with already made TrackerID, you can't create new one.
The items entered are community based so if people are messing up I can't do something about it at the moment... (Sadly)

The logic in place tell you what's is in logic :
► Green : In logic, everything is allright
► Yellow : You can be access it (following the logic) according to everything you got (even non-logic stuff)
► Red : This is definitivly outside of the logic
► Gray : This is an hint.
